Enabling Digital Lending Services to Farmers and Agri MSMEs

Farmer uses a combination of human interaction, alternative data, and machine learning to close the critical data gap that prevents financial institutions from lending and offering services to smallholder farmers.

We are building the largest agri input platform in Bangladesh. iFarmer app enables agri-input retailers to access quality products and services to serve their farmers better and expand their businesses.
